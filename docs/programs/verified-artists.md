# Verified Artists Program

The verified artists program is a way for users to identify official artists on the platform. This is intended to help prevent users from being scammed by fake artists. All of our programs have requirements for users to meet in order to be eligible.

### Requirements [^1]

_Don't meet these requirements? [Apply for the growing artists program](/programs/growing-artists)._

- Must have a certain amount of followers on an applicable platform [^2]
    - Nexirift: 100
    - Twitter: 1,000
    - Bluesky: 1,000
    - Instagram: 1,000
- Original artwork validation
- Required proof of creation (timelapses, WIP shots, etc.)
- No AI-generated artwork is permitted in this program.

[^1]: If you are joining this program via the Growing Artists Program, certain requirements, such as the follower count will be frozen for you.

[^2]: TikTok and YouTube not eligible due to the short videos gaining more traction. Follower counts are subject to change, existing verified artists will not be affected.

### Features

- Associated with the @Artists account
- Verified artist badge on profile
- Additional support from @Artists account
- Enhanced credibility on the platform [^3]
- ... and more!

[^3]: This does not mean that users in this program will have their visibility artifically boosted on the platform.
